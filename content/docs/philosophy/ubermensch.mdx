The Übermensch is Nietzsche’s ideal of a human who continuously overcomes himself and creates values after the collapse of absolute meaning, announced by the death of God.

## Say Yes to life

For Nietzsche,\
God is Dead.\
What is left is to\
**create your own values**.

Find your own path,\
do not follow what\
everyone is doing.\
**Be Unique**.

**Joyful affirmation**,\
like Joy Boy in One Piece.\
Hug tragedy, conflict\
and suffering.\
Dance with them.

Expand your mind,\
creativity and self-discipline.\
Become a child.\
**Life as a continuous process**.\
Stagnation equals decay.\
Challenge even your own\
beliefs, habits or ideas.

Love life as it is.\
Love yourself as you are.\
Accept your destiny.\
Amor fati.

Make music with your fate.\
paint a work of art\
with your experiences.\
Practice Tai Chi with everything.\
The Übermensch lives\
as an **artist of existence**.

> The Übermensch does not escape suffering — he dances with it.
> Nietzsche.

## The 10 Core Values of the Übermensch

1. **Life Affirmation (Yes-saying)**: Joyful affirmation.
2. **Self-Overcoming**: No final identity.
3. **Value Creation**: Creativity powerhouse.
4. **Amor Fati (Love of Fate)**: Even Suffering is meaning.
5. **Creative Will (Will to Power)**: Power as creative expansion.
6. **Playfulness and Lightness**: Laugh and dance with life.
7. **Independence from the Herd**: No need for approval.
8. **Integration of Instincts**: Instincts as guidance.
9. **Beyond Good and Evil**: beyond moral binaries.
10. **Eternal Recurrence Readiness**: Honor everything.
