### General Tips

1. *Tagging*: Use "@" to tag people when you need their attention on a specific point.
2. ***Conciseness***: Be concise but clear. Avoid long paragraphs that may be hard to follow.
3. ***Professionalism***: Maintain a professional tone. Jira comments may be read by various stakeholders, including managers, QA engineers, and even clients.
4. *Progress Updates*: Regular updates on what you've accomplished can be helpful. Example: "Completed the data collection module, moving on to data processing."
5. *Blockers*: If you're blocked by an external factor or a dependency, mention it.
6. *Technical Choices*: If you make an important technical decision that diverges from the original plan, mention it.
