On using AI,\
focus on amplifying\
or challenge your\
initial assumptions.

Strengthen your own voice\
and creativity by exposure\
to better arguments,\
sharper formulations and\
crazy examples.

The [Ubermensch](../../philosophy/ubermensch.mdx) would say:

> If an AI proposes better values, clearer distinctions, or more consistent plans than yours, why should loyalty to your first thought matter?

First, keep using the\
collective intelligence\
in your own way.

Then dance with AI.\
Ask it to **challenge** these\
initial ideas or plans.

In this way you will get\
into the habit of overcoming\
your own beliefs and even\
improve your critical thinking.

***

> What would you rather have?\
> a tool that **thinks for you**?\
> or a tool that **makes you think**?
