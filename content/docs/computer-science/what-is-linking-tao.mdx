FarmOrchestra was created to share all the knowledge related with the Open Source project FarmOrchestra. 
Inspire people to connect ideas and concepts to make a bridge between Tech, Farming and Music. 

## Philosophy

### Building in Public 

Sharing every tip, idea or concept needed to advance in an on-going Open Source project is something that could potentially inspire people to use tech for good, grow their own food or even start learning to play an instrument. 

FarmOrchestra will always be public so ideas may inspire others or help making some more progress. 

### Open Source

FarmOrchestra follows the Open Source principle of open code, open ideas and open discussion. Please feel free to add a comment, issue or start a discussion on some of the concepts mentioned here. 

### Linking Your Thinking 

As mentioned in the quick start, this resembles a Digital Garden. That is a term coined to those websites where ideas are interconnected even though they reside in different areas. 

Thanks to the power of Obsidian and Fumadocs, this documentation site is using links to connect ideas and provide better navigation. 

### Tao Te Ching

As seen in some of the notes, the style of writing is a bit different than other docs. 

This is due to the passion for taoism and the book Tao Te Ching. In that book, there is no need of saying too much, just the necessary to share the lesson, concept or idea in a few lines. 

### Sharing knowledge for the sake of Science

FarmOrchestra is a place to share everything related with Computer Science, Agriculture, Music or even Philosophy. 

This goes in line with the principle of sharing knowledge for making progress in Science. 

## When to use Fumadocs

FarmOrchestra is useful for those following on the progress of the project FarmOrchestra or even those who are interested in learning how to have fun by growing food.
